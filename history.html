    • History of AI

    • 1938-1946
      Science Fiction
      This was the Golden Era of exploring possibilities with technology. This ranged from fiction of how tech could work in the future to something as far fetched as time travel. Prominent figures such as John Campbell and E. E. "Doc" Smith reigned this era
    • 1956
      The First Mention of AI
      John McCarthy gathers a group of researchers to discuss the hypothetical of "Artifical Intelligence" and if it was possible. He was reportedly the first to coin the term AI.
    • 1970
      Flourishment
      Computers became faster and able to store more information. This was the start of machine learning algorithms. Marvin Minsky reported to Life Magazine, “from three to eight years we will have a machine with the general intelligence of an average human being.”
    • 1986
      AI in cars
      Navlab is the first autonomous car built. Being the first computer ran vehicle. The van had 5 rack of computer hardware, including 3 Sun workstations, video hardware and GPS receiver, and a Warp supercomputer.
    • 1997
      Autosmarting humans
      Deep Blue beats Garry Kasparov in chess after only 19 moves. This was the sixth game in total. Garry had won once, loss 2, and drawed for 3.
